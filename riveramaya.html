Chichen Itza History
Take a look at some of the greatest archeological sites in Mexico. These include astounding Chichen Itza located at a reasonable distance from the gorgeous white sand beaches of Cancun.

Xcaret Culture
The folklorik ballet in Xcaret presents the biggest theatrical showcase in the Yucatan peninsula. It takes you into a journey from prehispanic culture to what is now the culture of Mexico.

Aquariums, snorkel, diving Nature
Amaze yourself in the best cared coral reefs. Few other aquariums can pride in being built without destroying natural habitats while still showing visitors the wonders within their ecosystems.
